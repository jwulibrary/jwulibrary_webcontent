Welcome to the Booking page for the Study Rooms in the JWU Downcity and Harborside Libraries

Follow these steps to book a room.

  • Click on the link in the Room Bookings Box on the right hand side of this page that corresponds to the Campus Library in which you would like to book a Study Room
  • Click on the calendar to the left to pick your date.
  • Click on the available (green) blocks in the Scheduler to schedule a room, if the blocks are red the room is already booked for that time slot - fill out the form and you will receive e-mail confirmation for your booking -if you have any problems booking a room, please contact the library for help:

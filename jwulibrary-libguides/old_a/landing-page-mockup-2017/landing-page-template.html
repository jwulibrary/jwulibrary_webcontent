THE WORLD OF INFORMATION

Johnson & Wales University Library gives students the space and tools they need to navigate and synthesize today's complex world of information...

Header

providing access to a wide range of scholarly, literary and trade publications, including books,e-books, articles, images, videos, and websites plus the support of librarians and information specialists...

on campus or online...

Header

To support a curriculum dedicated to experiential learning, JWU Library supplies students with access to a wide array of top publications from their chosen industry, along with online training videos and professional certification test preparation manuals to help students get ready for life after graduation. Special collections such historic culinary artifacts and cookbooks, graphic novels, current best sellers in popular literature, plus a growing database of publications by Johnson & Wales University faculty and staff, set the stage for students to become sophisticated creators and consumers of information.
